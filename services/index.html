We don't use the term website much. To us, it sounds like "answering machine" or "floppy disk". It implies that the place you live on the Internet is a simple static presentation of facts; like a pamphlet or business card.

Websites are dead. Long live web apps!

Too many "business websites" present themselves like business cards and read like pamphlets. When a visitor finds you online, the last thing you want is to have them toss you into the virtual rolodex for later.

Your web application should be a dedicated, tireless, machine that performs all the necessary functions that your audience will need.
